The Flower, No. 1

2020
Digital composite
1080 pixels x 1080 pixels

The Flower. No. 1 is the first iteration of the text-based work conceived during the pandemic at the invitation to present works in response to the bicentennial of Women's Suffrage at the Boulder Museum of Contemporary Art in 2020. The work was published, one a day, for 69 days from March 28 - June 10, 2020.
