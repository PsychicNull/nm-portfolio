Forty-Seven Easy Poundcakes Like grandma Used To Make

2007 - 2012
Inkjet print digital drawing on paper
8 x 5 inches (20.32 x 12.7 cm)

Excerpt from Press release for Errata, PATRON gallery.

Forty-Seven Easy Poundcakes Like grandma Use To Make (2007-2012) is a series of forty-seven digital drawings. Morgan searched the web for “easy poundcake recipes” in the years after her own maternal grandmother's passing. Finding 46 distinct, published recipes, Morgan assembled the typed instructions for each, starting with her grandmother's—adding arrows and strike-throughs to indicate where each recipe diverged from the one previously listed. As a group, the works fracture apart from the definitive recipe. Intention, audience, and style are left to the wayside, the text divulges from their original use, and take on new potency.
