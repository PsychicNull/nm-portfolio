Like It Is: Drawings

2021 - 2023
Graphite pencil on coventry rag paper
38 x 50 inches/ ~97 cm x 127 cm (unframed); 41 x 53.25 inches (framed)

Benoît Blanchard, Gutenberg Project, JeanRoch Dard Gallery

The text of Extraordinary Popular Delusions and Madness of Crowds contained under its perfectly reproduced cover will not open for anyone. The volume disappears. Everything could end there, but the mimetic disillusion caused by the artist's hyper realistic technique also opens the door to an inﬁnity of rewritings. The work becomes - somewhat in spite of itself - a narrative starting point. [translated from French]
